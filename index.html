Empowerment Technologies

Using technology to empower individuals and communities

What is Empowerment Technologies?

Empowerment Technologies is the study and use of technological tools to enhance learning, communication, innovation, and productivity. It teaches students how to responsibly use digital tools to solve real world problems.

Main Goal

The goal of Empowerment Technologies is to empower students to become:

  • Responsible digital Citizens
  • Creative content Creators
  • Tech smart Learnes
  • Future ready Professionals

What you learn in this subject

Here are common topics:

  • Web design basics (HTML & CSS)
  • Word processing (like Microsoft Word)
  • Spreadsheets (like Excel)
  • Presentation tools (like PowerPoint)
  • Social media and online platforms
  • Internet safety & digital citizenship
  • ICT for academic and career development

Why it is important?

Empowerment Technologies is important because it equips students with digital skills, promotes responsible technology use, encourages creativity, and prepares them for future careers in a technology-driven world.
